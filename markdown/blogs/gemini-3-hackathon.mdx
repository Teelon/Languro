## Welcome to the Era of Gemini 3

Google DeepMind has invited the world to the **Gemini 3 Global Hackathon**, and Languro is answering the call. We are joining over 32,000 participants to push the boundaries of what AI can do by enhancing reasoning capabilities, unlocking multimodal experiences, and reducing latency.

This isn't just about building another chat interface. It's about creating next-generation applications. For Languro, that means solving the "intermediate plateau" in language learning with rigorous, AI-driven tools.

## The Challenge

**The Goal:** Build a NEW application using the Gemini 3 API.

**Why Participate?**
*   **Cutting Edge:** We are utilizing the newly released Gemini 3 API to build features that were previously impossible.
*   **Build Something New:** This challenge is strictly for new applications, aligning with our mission to overhaul traditional language learning.
*   **Win Big:** A prize pool of **$100,000** and opportunities with the AI Futures Fund.

## What We Are Building: The Anti-Duolingo

We are leveraging the **Gemini 3 family** to power our core modules, transforming Languro from a standard app into an adaptive language coach.

### 1. Smart Drill Engine (Powered by Gemini 3 Reasoning)
Static flashcards are dead. We are building a dynamic drill engine that uses Gemini 3's enhanced reasoning to:
*   **Generate Infinite Context:** Create unique sentences on the fly that force users to practice specific conjugations or grammar rules in varied contexts.
*   **Intelligent Diagnostics:** Instead of a red "X" when you fail, Gemini analyzes *why* you failed (e.g., "You used the subjunctive mood, but this clause requires the indicative because...") and instantly generates a new implementation drill to reinforce the correction.

### 2. Production Studio (Powered by Multimodality)
Language is meant to be produced, not just consumed.
*   **Multimodal Analysis:** Users submit audio recordings or written essays. Gemini 3 processes the raw audio to analyze pronunciation, intonation, and rhythm, while simultaneously critiquing the syntax and vocabulary choice.
*   **Latency-Free Coaching:** Thanks to Gemini 3's reduced latency, feedback feels instantaneous, mimicking the back-and-forth of a live tutoring session without the $50/hour price tag.
